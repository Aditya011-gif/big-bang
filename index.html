The Big Bang Theory

What is the Big Bang Theory?

The Big Bang Theory is the leading explanation of how the universe began. It suggests that the universe started as a single point, which expanded over billions of years to become the cosmos we know today.

Fun Facts

  • The universe is approximately 13.8 billion years old.
  • The Big Bang wasn't an explosion but an expansion of space itself.
  • Cosmic Microwave Background radiation is the afterglow of the Big Bang.
